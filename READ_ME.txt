Keys
-----

[Driving]
"."                       Accelerator
","                       Brake
"→"(aileron control)  　　 Turn to the right
"←"(aileron control)   　 Turn to the left
"M"                       Engages Reverse
"m"                       Disengages reverse

[Gun operation]
"↓"                  Turn the turret to the right
"↑"                   Turn the turret to the left
"5"　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Stop turning
"]"                       Turn the muzzle up
"["                       Turn the muzzle down
"e"                       Fire!(auto-repeat)
